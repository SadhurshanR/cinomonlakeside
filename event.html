Meetings & Events

The Cinnamon Hotel features over 2,000 sqm of meeting, convention, exhibition or wedding facilities with a grand ballroom and 11 function rooms (ranging from different sizes), ideal for events both intimate and large. Our venues are located across 3 floors (Level UC, G and 1) with escalator access and each offering elegant, modern foyers. Venues located on Level G are wheelchair accessible.


Our dedicated team of catering staff and professionals are able to help you plan your event or occasion every step of the way.
